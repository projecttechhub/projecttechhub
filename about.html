Our Story

Built by Engineers,
for Engineers

projecTech Hub was founded by a group of B.Tech graduates who remembered the stress and struggle of final-year project season — and decided to change that for future students.

Who We Are

From Hyderabad, Serving Students Across India

We started projecTech Hub in 2021 with one simple goal: make academic project development accessible, professional, and stress-free for every engineering student in India.

Our founding team includes IIT and NIT alumni, industry engineers from top tech companies, and passionate educators who have guided hundreds of students to top marks.

Today, we've delivered 500+ projects across 15+ domains and counting — from minor assignments to award-winning final-year major projects.
